GAME DEV

MIDNIGHT CROWN

STATUS: HIATUS
TIMEFRAME: 1 YEAR
DEC 2017 - JAN 2018

A WIP 'aerospace' combat game with full flight and combat controls both in atmosphere and zero-g. A passion project I have been working on as the sole developer.


FEATURES
  • Full 4 degree of freedom movement
  • Capital and fighter AI with behaviour trees and local avoidance
  • Diagetic cockpits linked with ship subcomponents
  • Trajectory prediction and aim assist for kinetic weapons
  • Missiles with predictive guidance
  • and much more...

Postponed for restructuring and iteration.


BUILT WITH: Unity, C#, Blender, FMODStudio, Adobe Suite
