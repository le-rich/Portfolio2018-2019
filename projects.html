MIDNIGHT CROWN

STATUS: HIATUS
TIMEFRAME: 1 YEAR
DEC 2017 - JAN 2018

A WIP 'aerospace' combat game with full flight and combat controls both in atmosphere and zero-g. A passion project I have been working on as the sole developer.


FEATURES
  • Full 4 degree of freedom movement
  • Capital and fighter AI with behaviour trees and local avoidance
  • Diagetic cockpits linked with ship subcomponents
  • Trajectory prediction and aim assist for kinetic weapons
  • Missiles with predictive guidance
  • and much more...

Postponed for restructuring and iteration.


BUILT WITH: Unity, C#, Blender, FMODStudio, Adobe Suite

ARBoreal

OCT 2017 - APRIL 2018

An AR research project I participated in under the supervision Dr.Michael Barton.

The intention of the project was to enable architectural visualization of structures before they are built, and then to use AR to assist in the construction process once designs are finalized. To be used both industrially and educationally. See here for more information.

I was responsible for building a cross software solution for transferring 3D data from an architectural prototyping software, Rhinoceros, to the Unity Game Engine in order to be rendered by the Microsoft Hololens. A thoroughly challenging and incredibly rewarding experience.


Special thanks to the School of Architecture and Landscape Architecture UBC.

BUILT WITH: Rhinoceros 5, C#, Microsoft Hololens, Unity3D

GAME DEV CURRICULA

STATUS: COMPLETE
TIMEFRAME: 4 MONTHS

A series of published books to instruct kids (and kids at heart) how to make games of increasing complexity. Each game has step by step instructions on how to make every single feature with in-depth explanations about every line of code. Challenge questions are also provided for additional difficulty.


THE BOOKS AND THEIR GAMES
  • PY101 - PY302: A series of 2D games of escalating complexity using Pixelpad.io
  • UN401 - CS701: A series of 2D and 3D games that challenges kids with the Unity3D Engine. I also worked on several Computer Science courses.

Thanks to Under the GUI Academy for the Opportunity.
